For the safety of all guests, staff and our community as a whole, you agree to the following policies. You should not visit any of the establishments on Servstry.com if you are unable or unwilling to comply with any of the policies and guidelines listed below.

Establishments on Servstry.com might have additional policies in place not listed below. Please contact the establishment prior to arrival if you are unsure you are able to comply with all listed and unlisted policies.

  • I have not been diagnosed with COVID-19. If so, I have been cleared by medical professionals to visit food and beverage establishments
  • I am not currently experiencing any of the symptoms known or thought to be associated with COVID-19. Please visit CDC.gov for additional information
  • I have not knowingly been in contact with any persons diagnosed with or thought to be experiencing symptoms of COVID-19
  • I agree to use a face covering when not eating or drinking
  • I agree to use hand sanitizer when entering the establishment and regularly wash my hands and/or use hand sanitizer while in the establishment
  • I agree to maintain physical distance from staff and other guests. I agree to avoid clustering within or in proximity to the establishment
  • I agree to avoid the unnecessary touching of the establishment’s surfaces
  • When applicable, I agree to use separately designated entrances and exits
  • When applicable, I agree to follow all separation and directional markers designated by the establishment
  • I agree, to the best of my ability, to adhere to all federal and local policies and regulations in reference to COVID-19
  • I will adhere to all of the establishment’s health and safety policies. I understand and agree that I may be refused entry to the establishment or will be removed from the establishment if I am unable or unwilling to follow these policies
  • Even with following all federal and local policies and regulations, I understand that there is an increased risk of exposure by participating in dine-in service. I agree to not hold the establishment and/or Servstry.com liable if I am to be exposed to and/or contract COVID-19 from visiting the establishment
  • I agree to notify Servstry.com, at my earliest convenience, if I am diagnosed with COVID-19 within two weeks of visiting any establishment on Servstry.com
